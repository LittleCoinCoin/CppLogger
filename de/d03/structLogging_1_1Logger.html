◆ logLevelFlag

Logging::LogLevelFlag Logging::Logger::logLevelFlag = Logging::LogLevelFlag_All
static

The log level flag to determine which log levels are enabled. By default all log levels are enabled.

To disable a log level, set the corresponding bit to 0. In such cases, messages of that will not be dispatched to the sinks.
